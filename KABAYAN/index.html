Kabyan, Benguet

One of the municipality in Benguet best known for for the antiquated centuries-old mummies and Mount Pulag

An ancient Ibaloi settlement known for its 500+ year-old fire mummies and as the home of Mt. Pulag. Originally settled by the Ibaloi at Imbosi, it became a major cultural center, later organized by Spanish colonizers in 1846 into rancherias including Adaoay and Lutab. The town is a recognized National Cultural Treasure for its burial caves.
