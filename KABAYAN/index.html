A Brief History of Kabayan, Benguet

Kabayan is renowned for its centuries-old fire mummies, an ancient burial practice unique to the Ibaloi people. These burial caves are recognized as National Cultural Treasures, preserving the heritage of the Cordillera region.

Beyond its cultural legacy, Kabayan is home to Mount Pulag, the highest peak in Luzon, famous for its “sea of clouds.” Today, Kabayan balances heritage preservation with sustainable tourism and community development.
